[Bio4j](http://bio4j.com/) is a bioinformatics graph based DB including most data available in [**Uniprot KB**](http://www.uniprot.org/) (SwissProt + Trembl), [**Gene Ontology**](http://www.geneontology.org/) (GO), [**UniRef**](http://www.ebi.ac.uk/uniref/) (50,90,100), [**RefSeq**](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/RefSeq/), [**NCBI Taxonomy**](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Taxonomy/), and [**Expasy Enzyme DB**](http://enzyme.expasy.org/).
